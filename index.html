Did you know that the Chiropractic profession is a regulated healthcare profession in South Africa by the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa. A doctor of Chiropractic looks to help patients who might be suffering with various muscle, joint or pinched nerve pain. A chiropractor likes to look at a person from all systems and does refer when needed to other healthcare professionals like a medical doctor, physiotherapist, biokinetist, podiatrist or occupational therapist in order to help you become healthy and achieve wellness.
