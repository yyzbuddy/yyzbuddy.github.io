How To Take Care of Your Mattress

Read below how to take care for your mattress.

  1. Use a mattress protector: Cover your mattress with a mattress protector, which will absorb perspiration and skincells and can be removed and washed to keep the mattress clean.
  2. Never flip or turn: Never turn your mattress.
  3. Room temperature: Mattress is best at room temperature of 23°C.
  4. Let it breath: If you detect a slight "new product" odour, leave the mattress and foundation uncovered and well ventilated for a few hours. A breath of fresh air should do the trick!
  5. Give it good support: Use a sturdy bedframe, ultimately use a Sleep Active, 14 slat, base with your mattress. This will ensure the mattress has adequate support that will prevent bowing or breaking.
  6. Don't dry clean: The chemicals in dry cleaning agents/spot removers may be harmful to the fabric or underlying materials. Vacuuming is the only recommended cleaning method. But if you're determined to tackle a stain, use mild soap with cold water and apply lightly. Do not ever soak a mattress or foundation.
